About Us

The Automation and Robotics Society(TARS) of IIIT BHUBANESWAR is a cult of techies and innovats run by the sophomores of our college, in a vision to develop and train the tech culture in our college and nurture the freshcomers for the upcoming future. Whether you lke to invent, enhance or modernize technology or just can't satiate your curosity about the mind-boggling magic of it, or simply have an inclination to this sphere, TARS 2.0 is where you will surely fell where you belong. from engrossing wrokshops to insightful sessions, guidance and mentoring by tech experts, loads of fun events and competitions, and a lot more await you at TARS, IIIT-BHBUBANESWAR.
